Hello! I'm Bella Nessim, a student at the University of Miami where I'm pursuing a degree in Creative Advertising and Marketing. This website serves as a canvas for my thoughts and analyses on art. Here, I share my writings that traverse through various epochs and styles, reflecting on how art continuously shapes and is shaped by the cultural currents of our time. Welcome to my space, where art meets commentary. I hope you find these pieces as enlightening and engaging as I find in creating them.
